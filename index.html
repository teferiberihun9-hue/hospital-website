About Mizan AMAN Hospital

Established in 1985, Mizan Aman Hospital is a leading referral hospital in the Bench Sheko Zone. We offer
a 200+ bed facility, intensive care, advanced chemical pathology lab, and a team of dedicated specialists.
Our mission is to provide equitable, high-quality healthcare with compassion and integrity.

  • Advanced Chemical Laboratory (hormone assays, toxicology)
  • 24/7 Ambulance & Trauma Care
  • Teaching & Research Partnerships
